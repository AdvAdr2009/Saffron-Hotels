Facilities

The Best Facilities for the best Stay

Swimming Pool

We have a half Olympic Size Outdoor Swimming Pool.

As per Covid-19 protocol, only 25 people are allowed at a time in the pool.

There are pool activities every weekend(activities are not disclosed in this website).

Beach

The hotel owns 1.5 km.sq of the beach with beach lounging chairs and we have beachside sports activities such as beach football and beach vollyball.

We have beach activities such as Kayaking, Banana Boat, Parasailing, Speed Boat, Ski Boats and so much more.

Sports Activities

We have a good quality tennis court and a tennis racket renting system for 100 rupees an hour for one racket.

We also have a volleyball court.

There is a kids play-area and park.

Gym

There is a fully equiped gym in the hotel.

In-Room Facilities

Open Kitchens

Every room has an LCD TV, telephone, free Wifi, attached bath with soaps, shampoos and towels and 24 hr hot water. An open kitchen with a refrigerator, microwave, electric kettle, a sink, and shelves with cutlery is also available. Snacks are also available but payable on use.

Swimming Pool(Only for Premium Rooms)

There is a private swimming pool in the backyard of every premium room. If you are willing to spend a little more money, then go for it
